Here's how SwiftPark works:

You sign up for free (there are no up-front fees) and post a sign (which we provide). You'll be in business within 72 hours.

When someone wants to park on your lot, they simply call or visit our site and pay quickly and conveniently, 24/7. This solution is suitable for all types of parking: short-term parking, longterm parking, event and venue parking, and more.

All lot activity, parking payment collections, and usage reporting is available securely, online.

When your Parking Enforcement provider patrols your parking lot, they simply log into the SwiftPark system with a secure ID to confirm which vehicles are authorized. Unauthorized vehicles may be subjected to being ticketed and/or towed away.

At the end of the month, we send you a check for the money earned that month.

SwiftPark instant paperless parking. It's quick, it's simple, it's convenient.
